A winding road to IMC

In September 2025, I started the Bachelor's program “Informatics” at IMC Krems. The path that led me here was anything but straightforward:

After graduating from HLUW Yspertal, a higher vocational school specializing in natural sciences and business, I moved to Vienna to work as a secretary in a law firm. There, I gained professional experience in the legal field, which led to a position in the legal department of the NÖ Landarbeiterkammer. The NÖ Landarbeiterkammer represents the interests of workers and employees in agriculture and forestry in Lower Austria - a very unique institution.

After two years of learning about Austrian labor law, payroll accounting, and the art of dispute resolution, I was offered the opportunity to switch to the IT department - an offer I gladly accepted. Suddenly, I found myself working with one colleague in a small IT department, with little prior knowledge in the field, and a lot of work to do. My tasks included managing databases, administering PCs and mobile devices, ensuring cybersecurity, and keeping our in-house server running, among other things. I loved the work, but after two years of trying to juggle all these responsibilities without a solid background in computer science, I realized it was time to pursue a comprehensive education in informatics. And, as far as I can tell, it was absolutely the right decision...
